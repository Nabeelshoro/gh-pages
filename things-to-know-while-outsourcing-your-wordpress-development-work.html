Things to know while Outsourcing your WordPress Development Work

Nabeel Shoro September 7, 2016 WordPress

Versatility is synonymous with WordPress. With tons of themes and plugins offered by this popular Content Management System, you can create an engaging and feature-rich websites without ever touching a single line of code. In fact, even an amateur with very little or no technical knowledge can create a website with it. However, when it comes to building a customized website with WordPress, you may require a comprehensive knowledge of PHP, HTML, and CSS. And of course, not everyone is familiar with these languages nor you can learn them in a day or two. Thus, outsourcing your WordPress development work to an offshore WordPress development company will enable you to bring your ideas to life.

These development companies are equipped with the right technical and programming skills, resources and tools required to build tailor-made and customized websites.

[mks_dropcap style="letter" size="52" bg_color="#ffffff" txt_color="#000000"]H[/mks_dropcap]owever, before you dive into hiring one of the WordPress development companies for your next online venture, there are certain things that you should keep in mind in order to find the most reliable and knowledgeable company.

1. How many years of experience they have?

The most important thing to consider while hiring a development company is to know their total relevant experience. Since WordPress is an extremely popular software and has given opportunities to many developers and programmers around the world, a number of amateur developers have also started offering their services as a professional WordPress developer. These amateur freelance developers may be good at developing a simple, basic WordPress website, but you cannot trust them with your project where a lot of customization will go into creating a beautiful piece.

Thus, it is always recommended to hire a company that holds the minimum of 3 years of relevant experience.

2. Has the potential company ever worked on a similar project before?

No two businesses are same. And of course, they will have different business needs and requirements. To ensure that the potential company will be the right fit for your project, it is important to do a little background check and ask them if they have ever worked on a similar project before. And if they have, ask them to show it. This way you can check whether the company is competent or not.

3. How was the experience of the previous clients?

If the potential company is in business for more than 3 years, it will surely have many clients and projects under its belt. These clients are the best way to know whether you should choose the company or not. Ask the company for some of its previous or existing clients contact number, or email ids. Consult these clients about their experience working with the potential company and disregard it if you hear more than 2 negative reviews.

Conclusion:

The most important thing to finding a reliable and trustworthy company for your next online venture is to make a list of potential companies offering best WordPress development services. Once you have created a list, start cutting the names of the companies that you find even slightly dishonest and incompetent.
