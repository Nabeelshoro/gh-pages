Ten Things You Did Not Know About WhatsApp

Another important fact is that Whatsapp recently started to encrypt the information we share through the application of protecting both hacker attacks and requests made by governments; still a law that imposed; Whatsapp could not help decipher messages.

And in spite of all these advances in instant communication and scope to which the company aims to reach seeking to offer the service on its Web version; most users Whatsapp could be missing out on some things that would improve their experience with this awesome app as not knowing most used tools offered.
