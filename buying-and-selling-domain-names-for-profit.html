GoDaddy - A Domain Marketplace For Domain Auction

GoDaddy is another marketplace for buying selling domains, where you can find the real name for your business and it is for this reason that the site deserves to be on this list. A large part of the domain is sold through auction, so there is a chance to get good offers.

NamePros - A Online Community For Buying And Selling Domains

NamePros is an online forum community, which has existed since the beginning of 2000, and is one of the most famous domain marketplaces. You can find not only experts and reputable buyers on this site, but you can also do some good domain names suggestion. If you are new in this industry and want to learn something about domain sell and purchasing method then NamePros is a right place to learn.

Catchy - A Marketplace For Domain Rent

The site offers "rent domain" on a contractual basis, which means that you can rent a catchy domain name for your business or start-up for about $ 1,000 a month. It's not a bad deal, considering that some domain names can end up costing hundreds of thousands of dollars, and it is safest to invest in something safe, but also considerably cheaper. If you have any domain name which is on requirements of Catchy.com, you can sell it at very good price.

Cax - Domain Name Marketplace cax.com
