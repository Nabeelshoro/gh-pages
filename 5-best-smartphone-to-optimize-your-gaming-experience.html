Here are five of the best smartphone in choosing your gaming smartphone

1. iPhone 6 Plus

The iPhone 6 Plus is an Apple product that has the largest size of all iPhone series and is suitable for playing games with a variety of genres. IPhone 6 Plus has the A8 processor which is able to create a graphic display games played at increased very significant. In addition, a large screen display also supports visual acuity games played so as to make the gaming experience becomes more real and exciting.

2. Google Nexus 6

Google Nexus 6 runs on the latest Android operating system, Android 5.0 Lollipop and is equipped with a 6-inch Quad HD display which is so rich so that pixels capable of displaying a graphic display is so sharp and perfect for gaming experience.
Equipped with a built-in multitasking function, this smartphone allows you to still be able to maximize the features available to perform other activities simultaneously during game play.

3. Sony Xperia Z3

Sony Xperia Z3 is a smartphone with 5.20 inch screen powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 processor and 3GB RAM.

This smartphone also has a camera that is so sharp, that is 20.7 megapixels. No Problem gaming performance, you can play through this smartphone with the quality of performance equivalent to Sony PlayStation.
