Microsoft stops Windows 7 and 8

Nabeel Shoro November 1, 2014 Computer News

On 31st October 2014, Microsoft has officially stopped selling their two operating systems, Windows 7 and Windows 8. So, what happens with a PC or laptop that is still marketed by bundling the Windows duo?

Discounts! Yes, starting yesterday all computer vendors are already not allowed to bundling their devices with Windows 7 Home Basic, Home Premium, and Ultimate editions. The same is true for Windows 8. As a result, PCs and laptops that are still sold by bundling the OS-OS was likely to be subject to a discount.

Given is now nearing the end of the year, products that have been subject to earlier discounts are also most likely to be subject to clearance program! The price is predicted to be much more 'skewed'.
