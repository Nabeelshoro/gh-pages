The 5 best laptops with dual screens

Nabeel Shoro November 23, 2020 review

In this article, we list the 5 best dual-screen laptops that adapt to different users.

Dual-screen notebooks are ideal not only for creative professionals, business people but also for gamers. Users get an additional screen to optimize their interaction with the interface. For example, users can navigate meetings at work or work with multiple applications and windows. Also, dual-screen notebooks have 4K, UHD, FHD displays, innovative designs, and come in a portable format.

However, there are some details to consider before purchasing a dual-screen laptop. An example is the size of the keyboard, since this is a dual-screen laptop, the keyboard may be smaller or even missing. It can be hard to get used to at first, but it provides portability through its lightness. Price is also an important factor. A laptop with two screens is more expensive than a regular laptop like the Asus 14 ”ZenBook Duo at around $ 1,500.

However, here are our best dual-screen laptops for office or gaming.

Asus ZenBook Pro Duo UX581

The Asus ZenBook Pro Duo has an optional 4K display known as the ScreenPad Plus. The dual-screen laptop from the Asus ZenBook Pro Duo is the main plus and its most visible feature. It has a 15.6-inch 4K OLED touchscreen with 3840 x 2160 resolution, which provides high image quality.

Also, it has a touch bar and an on-screen bar that can display entire applications. Its keyboard is located at the front of the lower half of the device, while its touchpad is located to the right of the keyboard.

Plus, they combine with powerful components to make them an expensive device. For example, a model with a 6-core Intel Core i7 9750H processor costs $ 2,699. Another version with an 8-core Intel Core i9 9980HK processor costs $ 3,299.

The Asus ZenBook Pro Duo UX581 is thick and heavy because of the dual-screen. Dimensions: 0.94 x 14.13 x 13 x 9.68 inches (2.4 x 35.9 x 24.6 cm) and weighs 5.51 lbs (2.5 kg).

Finally, some other specs are that it has an Nvidia GeForce RTX 2060 GPU, 8GB, 16GB, and 32GB RAM, and up to 1TB of storage.

Asus ZenBook Pro Duo UX481

The Asus ZenBook Pro Duo UX481 is a dual-screen laptop with high performance and a reasonable price. It has a 14-inch 1080p matte display and an optional 12.6-inch panel. The Asus ZenBook Pro Duo UX481 is ideal for video editors due to its high performance and long battery life. It is also a device for creative people and business professionals who need to optimize screen real estate.

Also, the main screen is impressive in terms of color saturation than usual and has a matte panel. The second screen occupies the top half of the Duo deck, and the keyboard is located on the front panel with a vertical touchpad.

Other specs for this device include an Intel Core i7 processor, 16GB of RAM, a 1TB HDD drive, Nvidia GeForce MX250 graphics, and Harman Kardon sound. Its price is $ 1,499 and weighs 1.8 kg.

Asus ROG Zephyrus Duo 15

This dual-screen Asus Rog Zephyrus Duo 1 5 laptop is one of the top gaming devices thanks to its efficient cooling and high gaming performance. It features an optional ROG ScreenPad Plus with powerful components to ensure compatibility with AAA games. Plus, this dual-screen laptop is powered by a Core i9 processor, 4K display, 48GB of RAM, 2TB of storage to match Nvidia GeForce RTX 2080 settings and graphics.

Zephyrus Duo 15 is thinner and more compact. It measures 0.82 x 14.2 x 10.6 inches (vertical) and weighs less than 5.3 pounds, making it a portable device. It has a blue steel color with a metallic finish.

Also, this device has a dual-screen: main and secondary. The main one is 15.6-inch, it can be a 4K 60Hz panel or a 1920 X 1080p 300Hz panel. The second display is integrated into the top half of the keyboard and has a 14.1-inch touchscreen with 60Hz and 3840 x 1100 resolution. The Zephyrus Duo 15 has an Adobe RGB color space that is more appealing to gamers. Also, Asus offers a 90W battery for extended use by gamers.

HP Omen X 2S

The HP Omen X 2S Dual Screen Notebook PC features a 15.6-inch Full HD LCD primary display. It has a 144Hz frequency and is Nvidia G-Sync compatible. The secondary screen has a 5.98-inch 1080p multi-touch screen with an RGB keyboard. This dual-screen laptop is packed with performance and graphics.

This device is ideal for immersive gaming. It has an Intel Core i9 processor, 32GB of RAM, 1TN SSD, and Nvidia GeForce RTX 2080 graphics with Max Q design. Plus, it has sound amplified by HP AUDIO BOOST 2.0 and DTS X ULTRA to enhance your gaming experience. you won't miss a beat of the sound while playing. This device weighs 5.18 pounds. and 0.78 ".

Lenovo ThinkBook Plus

Lenovo ThinkBook Plus is a multi-tasking laptop. The novelty of this dual-screen laptop is that you can take notes directly on the lid with the included stylus. You can even sync with OneNote and convert it to charts or text, saving you time, for example, during meetings.

In terms of specs, the Lenovo ThinkBook Plus is powered by a 10th Gen Intel Core i7 processor with 8GB RAM, 256m2 PCle SSD with 16GB Intel Optane memory, and Intel UHD Graphics. It features a 13.3-inch FHD monitor and a 10.8-inch e-ink display and has a long battery life (about 10 hours). Also, several safety features are included. For example, the fingerprint readers in the power button and the physical webcam shutter. Plus, it has Alexa integrated if you need it

Conclusion

If you are looking for a dual-screen laptop for gaming, we advise you to opt for the ASUS ROG Zephyrus Duo 15 or HP OMEN X 2S, which are excellent choices. However, if you're looking for a dual-screen laptop for your day-to-day work, the Asus ZenBook Pro Duo UX581 or the Asus ZenBook Pro Duo UX481 is your best bet.

If you enjoy multitasking more, get the Lenovo ThinkBook Plus. These are our recommendations, however, you need to consider your needs and budget before making your choice.
