The Samsung Galaxy S4 Deceiving Consumers Or Not

Nabeel Shoro September 4, 2014 Smartphones

Smartphones today are not rare items possessed only certain circles, but it has become a necessity anyone in the world. Moreover, in terms of purchasing a Smartphone consumer must be really careful in choosing a Smartphone before deciding to buy.
Storage space is already a sector that should be considered in addition to others. Therefore, consumers will use it to install applications, store music files and other files.
In a comparison test conducted by British technology site, it was decided that the Samsung Galaxy S4 into most Smartphones "deceive" consumers. Therefore, with the 16 GB model, the Smartphone was only providing free memory capacity under 10 GB, which is equal to 8,56 GB.

Meanwhile, Which do storage capacity ratio on the Samsung Galaxy S4, LG G2, HTC Mini, BlackBerry Z30, Sony Xperia Z1, the iPhone 5S, Google Nexus 5, and the iPhone 5C.
As a result, two new phones Apple entered the top three as a Smartphone which has a storage capacity of 16 GB. While the Galaxy S4 is in the final sequence.
Explained, 5C iPhone 16 GB version provides capacity allocation by 79 percent which can be used for users to save files or install applications.
In the second place there is the Google Nexus 5 with 12,28 GB iPhone 5S with 12,20 GB followed in third position.
Although some versions of the Smartphone provide a micro SD slot for memory expansion, the performance of external memory is not as good as the internal memory.
Here's a list of free capacity available Smartphone with 16 GB models:
1. iPhone 5C (12,60 GB)
2. Google Nexus 2 5 (12,28 GB)
