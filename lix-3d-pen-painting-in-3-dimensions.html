LIX 3D Pen - Painting in 3 Dimensions

Nabeel Shoro December 14, 2014 Technology Overview

We still have quite accustomed to 3D printers and able to paint and pens in 3 dimensions. Draw and write in the air (without needing paper) is now a reality. its only possible with LIX Pen. This 3D printing pen developed in London.

In practice, the pen functions are very similar to those of a 3D printer. LIX has different heat resistance plastic filaments printers thereof. However, its small size (measures 16.3 inches long by 1.3 thick and weighs only 35 grams) and feeding through a simple USB cable, do so completely different. Consider this video ( http://vimeo.com/90314569 ) to understand its potential.
