5 Important Reasons to use Android Emulator

Nabeel Shoro September 22, 2019 Technology Review

Nowadays, users have preferred to use mobile devices to desktop PCs. Surfing on the Internet is one of the most popular activities for which it is not necessary to sit in front of a computer or laptop. People publish status on social networks and launch games through their phone.

In this context, using Android emulators to play mobile games or run an application on a computer may seem counterintuitive. But in fact, the number of people using Android emulators is increasing. Why? Let's look at 5 reasons why a user may need to use an Android Emulator on their PC.

1. Android emulator gives you a bigger screen

Keeping a close eye on your phone harms your eye. This is certainly not very good for your health. Moving from a small screen of a telephone to a large screen of a computer raises its head, also, the large screen has a better display. This greatly improves your accuracy when pressed in games.

2. Saving the mobile phone battery

Cell phone batteries are small and lightweight for portability, but this also means that they cannot last very long. Powerful games heat-up your phone and the battery runs down very quickly. No one wants to lose the battle because your phone is down and disconnected at the very last moment. Using the Android emulator, you never have to worry about power. You can watch videos or stay connected in your favorite game for as long as you need.

3. Launch multiple applications or use multiple accounts at the same time.

Imagine that you could watch a video in any video sharing application without interrupting your conversation on WhatsApp or using multiple accounts for the game at the same time. That's fine, isn't it? Although not all Android emulators have this feature, with Nox App Player you can simultaneously run multiple instances of Android on your PC screen. Control your game in one of them, watch the video on the other and watch the conversation on WhatsApp on one big screen! 

4. The emulator allows you to replace the top-end Android phone

Mobile games are not just casual games. More and more hardcore games appear with a deep plot and complex gameplay. Accordingly, for a smooth and fast game, the graphics and display of the top device are required. The Android emulator will save you the budget to replace your phone since you can get for free this most “virtual, top-end Android phone” on your PC.

5. Access to mobile applications from a PC

With the growing number of smartphones and mobile networks, many applications meet the specific needs of people. However, some of them, such as Instagram, are designed to work only on mobile devices. For people who would like to access these services in a more “traditional” way from a PC, an Android emulator is a great solution.
