How to hide your WhatsApp Profile Photo for one or more contacts

Nabeel Shoro January 13, 2020 WhatsApp New Features
In this article, we will introduce you how to have more privacy when using the messaging app WhatsApp. 
WhatsApp no doubt is one of the important messaging platforms to text, audio or video call to your phone book contacts. in case of privacy if you want to hide your profile picture one or more contacts, here The Tricks Peak Tips is going to introduce on a new coming feature of WhatsApp by which you can sate your privacy according to your need.

What version of WhatsApp has Hide WhatsApp Profile Photo for one or more contacts?

This feature is available in all versions of the smartphone app with Android and iPhones after 2020. 

How to use the feature of hiding WhatsApp Profile Photo for one or more contacts.

The way to do this is as follows:
- Access the settings menu (icon with three dots in the upper right corner of the Android app interface; Settings option in the lower-left corner on iPhone);
- Go to the item "Account";
- Click on "Privacy";
- Select “Profile Picture”
- Choose who the photo should be visible to everyone, your contacts, or nobody
To hide your WhatsApp profile picture for a specific contact, you need to check the photo visibility option only for your contacts, select the person's number, and delete it from your phonebook.
